Meet Me The Creator

I am a creative developer who lives at the intersection of clean code and cozy aesthetics.

My philosophy is simple: technology should be as delightful and approachable as a kitten's "mew." With a deep love for frontend architecture and a sharp eye for vibrant design, I specialize in crafting interactive experiences that turn simple information into a visual journey.

My work, exemplified by projects like NyaNeko, is fueled by a mission to blend educational value with high-energy UI, utilizing modern tools like Tailwind CSS and responsive layouts to ensure that every user feels right at home.

Whether I’m tinkering with glassmorphism effects, fine-tuning hover transitions that "pop," or curating a perfect palette of pinks and ambers, I am driven by the desire to build digital spaces that are as functional as they are fun.

Beyond the editor, I’m a community-oriented creator who values connection—whether that's through a shared love for feline facts or building a collaborative space for cat enthusiasts everywhere. I don’t just build websites; I build tiny, sunlit corners of the web for people to discover something new and leave with a smile.
